About the College of Engineering Technology



The College of Engineering & Technology offer Bachelor Degree's in five Engineering Disciplines at William V.S. Tubman University; Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. The College of Engineering & Technology is dedicated to empower future engineers of both gender with quality education and creative ability to advance industrial modernization and technological development.
